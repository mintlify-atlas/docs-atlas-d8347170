## What is a team?

A `Team` is identified by its **name** — a string, not a numeric ID. This means `"LegoStars"` is the canonical reference to a team across every API call, URL path, and relation.

### Fields

| Field | Type | Constraints | Description |
|-------|------|-------------|-------------|
| `name` | `String` | 3–50 chars, primary key | Unique display name and identifier. |
| `city` | `String` | max 100 chars | City the team is based in. |
| `foundationYear` | `Integer` | ≥ 1998 | Year the team was founded. |
| `educationalCenter` | `String` | max 100 chars, optional | Affiliated school or educational centre. |
| `category` | `String` | required | Competition category (e.g. `"Challenge"`). |
| `inscriptionDate` | `LocalDate` | past or present | Date the team was registered in the system. Set automatically on creation. |
| `members` | `List<TeamMember>` | max 10 | Individual participants on the team. |
| `trainedBy` | `Set<Coach>` | max 2 | Coaches assigned to the team. |
| `floaters` | `Set<Floater>` | max 2 | Floater volunteers supporting the team. |
| `registeredEditions` | `Set<Edition>` | — | Editions this team has been registered to. |

<Info>
Because `name` is the primary key, team names must be globally unique. Choose carefully — renaming a team is not supported.
</Info>

## Team members

A `TeamMember` belongs to exactly one team. Each member has:

| Field | Type | Constraints |
|-------|------|-------------|
| `id` | `Long` | auto-generated |
| `name` | `String` | 2–100 chars |
| `birthDate` | `LocalDate` | must be in the past |
| `gender` | `String` | max 20 chars, optional |
| `tShirtSize` | `String` | max 10 chars, optional |
| `role` | `String` | max 50 chars, required |

A team can have at most **10 members**. Adding an 11th throws an error.

## Coaches

A `Coach` can train multiple teams and a team can have multiple coaches, subject to these limits:

- A team can have at most **2 coaches**.
- A coach can train at most **2 teams**.

| Field | Type | Constraints |
|-------|------|-------------|
| `id` | `Integer` | auto-generated |
| `name` | `String` | required |
| `emailAddress` | `String` | valid email, unique |
| `phoneNumber` | `String` | optional |

<Warning>
Assigning a coach to a third team (or assigning a third coach to a team) is rejected with `MAX_TEAMS_PER_COACH_REACHED` or `MAX_COACHES_PER_TEAM_REACHED` respectively.
</Warning>

## Registering a team to an edition

Team registration is the act of enrolling an existing team into an open edition. You cannot register teams to editions in `DRAFT` or `CLOSED` state.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Ensure the edition is OPEN">
    Transition the edition from `DRAFT` to `OPEN` using the lifecycle endpoint. Registration is blocked in any other state.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create the team">
    Create the team resource at `POST /teams` if it does not already exist.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Register the team to the edition">
    Call `POST /editions/{editionId}/teams/{teamName}`. On success, the response returns `201 Created` with status `"REGISTERED"`.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Registration constraints

| Condition | HTTP status | Error code |
|-----------|-------------|------------|
| Edition not found | `404` | `EDITION_NOT_FOUND` |
| Team not found | `404` | `TEAM_NOT_FOUND` |
| Team already registered | `409` | `TEAM_ALREADY_REGISTERED` |
| Edition has 18 teams | `409` | `MAX_TEAMS_REACHED` |
| Edition is not `OPEN` | `422` | `EDITION_OPERATION_NOT_ALLOWED` |
